## General preferences

The **Settings → General** section contains appearance, language, and launch behavior controls.

| Preference | Options | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Language | English / Chinese | Applies to the entire UI immediately |
| Appearance | System / Light / Dark | Follows the `ui.appearance` UserDefaults key |
| Launch at login | Toggle | Registers Typeflux as a login item |

## Audio settings

Found under **Settings → Audio**.

| Preference | Options | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Preferred microphone | Automatic / any connected input device | Defaults to the system default input; falls back to Automatic if the saved device is disconnected |
| Mute system output during recording | Toggle | Suppresses system audio playback while the microphone is active |

## Advanced settings

| Preference | Default | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Sound effects | On | Plays audio feedback on recording start and stop |
| Persona hotkey applies to selection | Off (Beta) | When enabled, pressing the persona hotkey while text is selected opens the picker to rewrite that selection |
| Automatic vocabulary collection | On | Suggests new vocabulary terms from transcription results |
| Apple Speech fallback | Off | Falls back to on-device Apple Speech Recognition if the primary STT provider fails |
| Agent Framework | Off (Beta) | Must be enabled for MCP tools to be invoked during ask-selection |

## History retention

Control how long Typeflux keeps your transcription history under **History**.

| Policy | Retention period |
|---|---|
| Never | Records are deleted immediately after each session |
| 1 Day | Records older than 24 hours are purged |
| 1 Week | Records older than 7 days are purged *(default)* |
| 1 Month | Records older than 30 days are purged |
| Forever | No automatic deletion |

<Note>
  All history is stored locally in `~/Library/Application Support/Typeflux/history.sqlite`. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud.
</Note>

## Vocabulary

The Vocabulary section lets you maintain a list of custom terms that are passed to the STT provider to improve transcription accuracy for domain-specific words, names, and jargon.

Terms have two sources:

- **Manual** — entered by you directly via the **+ New Word** button.
- **Automatic** — suggested by Typeflux after it detects repeated or uncommon words in your transcription results (requires Automatic vocabulary collection to be enabled).

You can filter the vocabulary list by source (All / Automatic / Manual) and search by term. Duplicate terms are deduplicated automatically based on normalized (whitespace-trimmed) matching.

<Tip>
  Add product names, technical abbreviations, and proper nouns to the vocabulary list to reduce transcription errors for those specific words.
</Tip>
